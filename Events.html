Our Upcoming Rallies

Winter Driving Tests

Date - 15 to 17 October 2022

Place - North Africa

Event Organizer - Historic Adventure

×

The Great Escape, a tour organised by Historic Adventures, is taking place in the Western Cape from 8 – 15 October 2022. The Great Escape is a relaxed tour through some of the Western Cape’s most scenic roads, while staying in premium hotels & lodges. You will share a unique driving experience with likeminded people and use your vehicle in the way it was intended…to be driven. While there will be a suggested route, participants are welcome to deviate at any point and stop wherever they choose. We also have some exclusive experiences planned along the way! The tour is open to all pre 1985 vehicles. Your entry includes 6 nights of accommodation, breakfasts, dinners & complimentary wine, several lunches, a welcome dinner in Cape Town, exciting excursions and more! Entries are limited to 25 vehicles so don’t delay if you wish to join us!

Place - France GARD

Event Organizer - Model Events

×

The event which runs from 10am until 5pm on both days will attract well over one hundred boats which will be moored along the historic Stourbridge Canal. There is free admission to the site and there are a number of attractions which include classic vehicles, traction engines, amusements, canal boat trips, fairground organs and a craft fair in the Bonded Warehouse. The Isle of Man needs little introduction to petrolheads of any persuasion. For more than a century its unyielding and constricted lanes have hosted two and four wheeled competitions at the very highest echelons of world motorsport.
